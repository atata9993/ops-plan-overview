Reason for Plan

Our plan is all about improving our support operations to make things easier for everyone. We’ve been focusing on general IT support, but we realize that we need to be more accessible to the elderly, who might not always have the help they need.

Mission

To empower the elderly by providing accessible and effective tech support.

Vision

To build an inclusive tech community for confident technology use.

SMART Goals

1. Decrease Response Time to Under 5 Minutes

User benefit: Users get help faster, reducing wait times.

2. Reduce Ticket Backlog by 50% (400 to 200)

User benefit: Shorter queues mean quicker resolutions.

3. Increase First Contact Resolution Rate by 7%

User benefit: Issues resolved on the first call saves time.

4. Improve Customer Satisfaction from 85% to 95%

User benefit: Higher satisfaction means better communication and support.

5. Reduce Average Incident Resolution Time to Under 1 Hour

User benefit: Faster resolutions allow users to get back quickly.
